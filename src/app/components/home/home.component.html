Hire the IT expert you need - for any period of time - at low fees.

Flexperts helps companies to instantly find the IT expert they are looking for and to overcome short term staff shortage at a much lower commission than the usual 20% percent. A software automates the recruitment process, contract signing / extension / termination, time tracking and billing which are all usually manually done and therefore time consuming. Creating transparency and automating these steps reduces time to a minimum and keeps costs down.

Why flexperts?
